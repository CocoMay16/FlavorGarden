What is Flavor Garden?

A place to share food exploration and recipes. I love food, esspecially food with flavor. I had grown up always having home cooked meals and never eating out. My family had several gardens and animals so we had what we needed to make delicious food at home. I love food with color and flavor. My boyfirend is in the food industry and has exposed me to many different new foods and new restaurants. I even speant the summer working on Nantucket in a restaurants as a cook! This gave me and isiders look on some of the most expensive restaurants in America and what went into making it all come toegther. It left me loving the art of creating plates even more then when I started. I have created this page to documment food I've tried, want to try and have created. Enjoy!
